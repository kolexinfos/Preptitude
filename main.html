Welcome to Preptitude.

Preptitude is an interactive e-learning application which can be used (on-the-go) across platforms (on any device of your choice be it mobile device or computer system). It is used to aid preppers boost their performance by simulating the exam condition for the respective tests.The app. tries to simulate real-time experience with few adjustment with an attempt to help users effectively measure their performance with the real life experience. The name preptitude is a contraction of two key words which represents the basis of the app. 1st (prep-)aratory which the app does by aiding users preparation on the go with timed condition, progress review and prompt solution for correction and adjustment to help foster an effective preparatory ethic and the 2nd at(-titude-) which the app. does by pre conditioning and fine tuning test-takers attitude by giving them a sense of confidence and a positive expectation before they take the actual test. We envisage simulating most standardize exams within africa and beyond.
